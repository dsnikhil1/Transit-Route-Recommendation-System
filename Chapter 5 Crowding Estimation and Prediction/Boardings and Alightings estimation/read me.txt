Target: To get the boardings and alightings at each stage for all the trips on 3-11-2016
Background: Ticket data on a given day consists data of all trips between different O-D pairs made on that day

Input: Merged ticket data file of 3-11-2016
Output: Boardings and Alightings at each stage for all the trips on that day

Scope: The Stages list is obtained from the ticket data. For a schedule, all unique stages are collected from all the trips which gives the stage list for that schedule
